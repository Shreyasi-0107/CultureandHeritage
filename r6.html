Agra!!


DESCRIPTION

Agra is a historic city located in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is known for being the home of the iconic Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Agra is also famous for its rich culture, delicious cuisine, and traditional handicrafts. The city has a long history, with many significant events and figures contributing to its cultural and architectural heritage.
